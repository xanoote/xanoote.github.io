The Fair Share

What is FairShare?

FairShare is a free, easy-to-use expense splitter that helps households and roommates divide shared costs fairly based on income ratios. Instead of splitting bills equally, FairShare calculates proportional contributions that reflect each person's financial contribution to the household.

Why Fair Split Matters

When household expenses are split fairly based on income, it creates financial harmony and reduces arguments. Traditional equal splits can create resentment when one person earns significantly more but pays the same amount. FairShare ensures everyone contributes proportionally to their ability, fostering trust and reducing financial stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does FairShare work?

Simply enter each household member's income, add your shared expenses (rent, utilities, groceries, etc.), and FairShare automatically calculates each person's fair contribution based on their income percentage.

Why not split everything equally?

Equal splits work when incomes are similar, but can cause unfairness when incomes differ significantly. If one person earns 60% of household income but pays 50% of expenses, they're effectively subsidizing others. FairShare prevents this by ensuring proportional contributions.

What expenses should be shared?

Typically shared expenses include rent/mortgage, utilities, internet, groceries, household supplies, and insurance. Personal expenses like clothing, entertainment, or individual phone bills are usually not shared.

How does this reduce arguments?

By making the math transparent and fair, FairShare eliminates debates about "who pays what." Everyone can see the calculations are objective and based on agreed-upon income ratios, reducing resentment and financial disagreements.

Is this only for couples?

Not at all! FairShare works for any household size - couples, roommates, families, or any group sharing expenses. You can add as many people as needed.

What currencies are supported?

FairShare supports USD, EUR, SEK, NOK, and DKK, with automatic locale detection. The app uses proper currency formatting for accurate financial calculations.

Is my data private?

No data you fill in here is sent anywhere. All calculations are performed locally within your browser, ensuring your financial information remains completely private and secure.
